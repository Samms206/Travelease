Nusa Penida is a small island located southeast of Bali, Indonesia. Known for its natural beauty and stunning landscapes, Nusa Penida is a popular destination for travelers who are seeking a quieter and more secluded island experience. The island is surrounded by crystal clear waters that are perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and diving.

One of the main attractions of Nusa Penida is the stunning Kelingking Beach, which features a unique rock formation that resembles a T-Rex. Another popular spot is the Angel's Billabong, a natural pool with clear turquoise waters that are perfect for swimming.
